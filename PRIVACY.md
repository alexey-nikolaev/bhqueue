# KlubFlow Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Overview

KlubFlow ("the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### From Reddit (via Devvit app)
- **Public comments and posts** from queue update threads
- **Reddit usernames** (for attribution only)
- **Timestamps** of posts/comments

We only collect publicly visible content from posts tagged with queue-related flairs.

### From Telegram
- **Public messages** from the berghainberlin group
- **Telegram usernames** (for attribution only)
- **Timestamps** of messages

### From App Users (optional)
- **Email address** (if you create an account)
- **Queue position updates** (if you choose to submit them)
- **Approximate location** (only when actively submitting, with your permission)

## How We Use Data

- **Aggregate queue information** to estimate wait times
- **Display attribution** for crowd-sourced updates
- **Improve accuracy** of our estimates over time

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Private messages
- Personal information beyond what you voluntarily provide
- Precise location tracking
- Browsing history

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Aggregated, anonymized statistics may be shared publicly.

## Data Retention

- Public Reddit/Telegram data: Retained for historical analysis
- User accounts: Until you request deletion
- Location data: Deleted after processing (not stored)

## Your Rights

You can:
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Opt out of location-based features
- View what data we have about you

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, contact the developer through the app's GitHub repository.
